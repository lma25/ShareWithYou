Share With You Project

Looking around my room, I find I don't usually use most of them. The items I use everyday are just one chair, one table, one laptop and one cup. But I still need stuffs like a PlayStation, a vacuum cleaner and a suit. Although I may use them only once every month, I have to pay the same money for them as people who use them a lot. By sharing them with people neayby, everyone could gain benefit. Thus I build this project to let people share their belongings with others.

Hope you enjoy the web and the ShareWithYou live style :)
